Rules and Regulations

General Rules

  • Disrespecting CO or BOSS results in immediate kick
  • Toxic behavior with members results in kick
  • No response = 3 warnings
  • Must maintain a powerful connection
  • No message spamming
  • GBK misuse is prohibited
  • Radio noise is strictly forbidden

Operational Guidelines

  • Non-provocation policy towards other gangs and citizens
  • Rank progression based on performance and city activity
  • Promotion requests result in demotion
  • Gang departure requires valid reasoning
  • Mandatory VLR role in Discord
  • Whitelist requirement in PW
  • Comprehensive knowledge of RP and gang rules required
